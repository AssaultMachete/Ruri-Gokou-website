Appearance

Ruri's choice of fashion is frequently gothic lolita, always dressed in a white frilly top with a black bolero worn over it.

Pesronality

Ruri often switches her personality according to the character she poses (i.e., Shironeko, Kuroneko, Yamineko, etc..) being overly confident of her own atitude.
In reality, she is socially insecure, and usually uncomfortable being in crowded places.
Eventually, she made her small circle of friends.
